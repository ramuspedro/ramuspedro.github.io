Intro

I consider myself as learner and that's my passion. All the time I try to learn something new.

As a front-end developer I have been looking for increase my knowledge mainly in javascript, HTML and CSS. Inside these technologies there a incredible world to explore and all the time I am discovering new features.

As a back-end developer I've been looking for building systems with nodejs (js again). But now in Terras App Solutions PHP and Django 2.1 become very present everyday.

As a designer I have been looking for creating UI interfaces in many fake projects. I am learning a lot in this area. I hope someday I can consider myself a good designer.

Now I am going deep in Artificial Intelligence world. Concepts of Machine Learning and Deep Learning are become familiar to me.

My goal is learning and share everything with people.
